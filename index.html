Biography


My name is Shannon Thouvenin, and I originally hail from Massillon, Ohio. In my pursuit for knowledge and opportunities for growth, I moved to Columbus in 2010. I currently reside in Westerville, and have been dedicating my energies full-time since March, to pursue a great passion of mine; Front-end development. I want to create unique and beautiful webpages, that can exemplify a clients vision that far exceeds expectations. Utilizing the resources available, I have been dedicating my time and energy learning all that I can about development. I have learned HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, Git and have learned to write basic JavaScript. I have much yet to learn, but I am determined to put my skills on show. There are many resources at my fingertips, and I have had the privilege of diving into them. I have completed courses with the University of Michigan, Johns Hopkins University and Hong Kong University of Science and Technology that focused on front-end development. Codecademy has been another beneficial resource that is available and offers many topics that I am thrilled to explore. I have also made it a priority to attend "Girl Develop it" meetups as well as educational lectures through varying organizations, such as Pillar Technologies.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world". This is a quote by Nelson Mandela, and I whole-heartily agree. In 2013 I received my masters degree in Music Performance with an emphasis on Music History. I've always looked to help those around me grow and develop as they advanced in their career paths.
